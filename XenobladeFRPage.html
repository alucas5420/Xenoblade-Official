Overview




This expansion to Xenoblade Chronicles 3, known as Xenoblade Chronicles 3: Future Redeemed, was created by Monolith Soft and released by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. It was first hinted at at the Nintendo Direct on February 8, 2023, and then formally revealed on April 18, 2023. In North America, it was made available on April 25, 2023, and in other regions on April 26, 2023, as part of Wave 4 of the Xenoblade Chronicles 3 Expansion Pass.
