Overview




A stand-alone game created by Monolith Soft for the Nintendo Switch, Xenoblade Chronicles 2: Torna ~ The Golden Country is an expansion of Xenoblade Chronicles 2. It was initially revealed during E3 2018 at Nintendo's presentation. On September 14, 2018, it was first made available as a part of the Xenoblade Chronicles 2 Expansion Pass. On September 21, 2018, it was made available separately. Download codes for the Expansion Pass are also included with the solo version.
