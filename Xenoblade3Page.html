Overview




The role-playing video game Xenoblade Chronicles 3, was created by Monolith Soft and released by Nintendo for the Nintendo Switch. On February 9, 2022, the game was revealed at a Nintendo Direct event. On July 29, 2022, the game went on sale worldwide.

Xenoblade Chronicles 3 is the third main entry in the Xenoblade Chronicles trilogy.
